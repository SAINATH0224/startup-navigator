Our Story

Built for Aspiring Founders

Startup Navigator was created to solve a real problem: startup information in India is scattered across hundreds of government websites, legal documents, and blog posts. We've organized it all into one clean, searchable, AI-powered platform — completely free.

Mission

Why We Exist

Every year, hundreds of thousands of people try to start a business in India. Most give up not because their idea is bad, but because navigating the bureaucracy, legal requirements, and funding landscape is overwhelming.

We believe that with the right information and guidance, anyone with a good idea and the determination to execute can build a successful startup.

Startup Navigator is our contribution to making that possible — a comprehensive, free, AI-powered guide to everything startup founders need to know.
